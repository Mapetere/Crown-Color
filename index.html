Healthy Hair

Premium Care & Maintenance

Keep your style fresh and your natural hair healthy with our expert guide

Night Routine

Always sleep with a silk or satin bonnet to prevent frizz and maintain moisture in your braids.

  • Use a silk/satin pillowcase for double protection
  • Avoid tight ponytails while sleeping
  • Secure hair in a loose bun or pineapple

Scalp Hydration

A healthy scalp is the foundation of hair growth. Keep it hydrated and itch-free.

  • Apply a lightweight oil 2-3 times a week
  • Use a soothing scalp spray for itch relief
  • Avoid heavy greases that cause buildup

Washing & Refresh

You can clean your braids without ruining the style. Focus on the scalp and dry thoroughly.

  • Use diluted shampoo or a mousse cleanser
  • Pat dry with an old t-shirt (less frizz than towels)
  • Ensure roots are completely dry to avoid "braid mold"

Edge Protection

Protect your delicate hairline from tension and breakage while wearing protective styles.

  • Don't pull edges too tight in updos
  • Use a soft edge control without alcohol
  • Massage edges daily with castor or jojoba oil
